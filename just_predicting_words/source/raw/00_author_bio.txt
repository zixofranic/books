VERSION 1: THE STANDARD BIO (about 90 words)
Best for: Amazon listing, book jacket back flap
----------------------------------------------------------------

Ziad El Feghali is a technologist, educator, and entrepreneur 
with two decades of experience in game design, education, and 
technology. He co-founded Wixel Studios in Beirut and worked 
on Antura and the Letters, an award-winning Arabic literacy 
game for refugee children developed through an international 
consortium. He has taught at four universities in Lebanon and 
currently teaches Digital Publishing and Game User Experience 
at the Holy Spirit University of Kaslik. He is the technical 
co-founder of simplerOS, based in Louisville, Kentucky.

VERSION 2: THE LONGER BIO (about 140 words)
Best for: book jacket back panel, "About the Author" page
----------------------------------------------------------------

Ziad El Feghali is a Lebanese-American technologist, educator, 
and entrepreneur. He co-founded Wixel Studios in Beirut, where 
he spent fifteen years as Managing Founder working on games and 
educational products. He contributed game design work on Antura 
and the Letters, an Arabic literacy game for refugee children 
developed through an international consortium across Lebanon, 
Spain, and Germany. He was named one of Executive Magazine's 
Top 20 Lebanese Entrepreneurs in 2012 and received the EY 
Interactive Young Entrepreneur Award in 2010. He has taught at 
four universities in Lebanon, including ongoing courses on 
Digital Publishing and Game User Experience at the Holy Spirit 
University of Kaslik. He is the technical co-founder of 
simplerOS, a property-technology platform based in Louisville, 
Kentucky. He works in Arabic, English, and French.
