STYLE CONFORMANCE
================================================================

Voice: smart friend over coffee, conversational, example-rich.
"I" available but reader-centered.
Neutral American English throughout.

ZERO em dashes (—) across all 21 chapters and 3 appendices.
ZERO en dashes (–) across all 21 chapters and 3 appendices.
Verified with grep -c on every file.

All chapters in target word range: 2,500-3,700 words.
All chapters end with a bridge line to the next.
All foundational chapters have audio production notes.

All real people referenced by their commonly-known names, with 
no abbreviated initials anywhere in the book.
