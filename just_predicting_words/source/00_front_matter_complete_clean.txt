                    A Note on How This Book Was Made

  Before we begin, you deserve to know how this book came
  together.

  The research was assisted by AI. Some of the writing,
  editing, and corrections were done with the help of AI.
  Without these tools, I could not have written a current
  book about a field that updates in days, not years. By
  the time I finished a chapter the old way, the chapter
  would already be out of date.

  Several different language models and tools were used
  along the way. Some were better at research. Some were
  better at drafting. Some were better at catching the
  small errors that a tired human eye misses at midnight.

  But one human gathered the pieces. One human decided what
  this book is about, what voice it speaks in, what it
  argues, what it leaves out, and what it says directly to
  you. The structure, the judgment, the responsibility, are
  mine. That part will always be a human. It is, after all,
  what this book is finally about.
